Frequently Asked Questions

Is the service really free?

Yes! The basic version of our platform is completely free for all users. You can search for facilities, view information, and get directions without paying anything.

How accurate is the medicine availability?

Stock information is provided directly by pharmacy owners and updated in real-time. However, we recommend calling ahead for confirmation, especially for urgent medications.

Can I list my facility?

Absolutely! Business owners can register their facilities through our signup process. Your listing will be verified by our admin team before going live.

How do I get directions?

Simply click on any facility marker on the map, then click "Get Directions." The system will calculate the best route from your current location.

Is my location data private?

Yes, we take privacy seriously. Your location is only used to provide relevant search results and is never shared with third parties.

What areas do you cover?

Currently, we focus on Yaoundé, but we're expanding to other cities in Cameroon. Contact us if you'd like us to add your city.
